What is "Great Work"?
•
[1] “Doing great work means doing something important so well that you expand people's 
ideas of what's possible. There's no threshold for importance. It's a matter of degree, and 
often hard to judge at the time anyway. I'd rather people focused on developing their interests 
rather than worrying about whether they're important or not. Just try to do something 
amazing, and leave it to future generations to say if you succeeded.”

===== PAGE 33 =====
Term Project Evaluation Criteria (Draft)
•
Is AI-Native?
•
1: AI is cosmetic; the product works fine without it.
•
5: The product was impossible before LLMs; AI is the reason it exists.
•
So What?
•
1: No evidence anyone outside the team cares.
•
5: Users actively return to it; expands what people think is possible.
•
What's New?
•
1: A clone of an existing product.
•
5: Opens up a problem space no one was addressing.
•
Why You?
•
1: The team picked this because it seemed easy or safe.
•
5: The team's unique background or weird obsession makes them the best team.
