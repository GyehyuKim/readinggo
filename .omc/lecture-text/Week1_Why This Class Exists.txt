===== PAGE 9 =====
Problems
•
Noticed that managers/leaders generally have two different perspectives:
•
Some say "I tried. It doesn’t work."
•
Other say "AI can do everything."
•
Why?
•
AI evolves very quickly. Their experiences could be outdated.
•
Some heard of buzz, but they don’t have firsthand experiences.
•
Neither of the two is true.
•
The truth is usually somewhere in between.
