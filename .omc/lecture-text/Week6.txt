===== PAGE 18 =====
Never Commit Secrets — The Real Cost
19
•
.env stays local and goes in .gitignore
•
Leaked API keys get stolen by scraper bots
•
++ Real risk: four-figure bills on your card
•
Defenses that work: spending caps
•
Survey: 7 of 15 teams missing a root .gitignore — fix today
•
~23.8M secrets leaked on GitHub in 2024 [1]
[1] https://www.gitguardian.com/state-of-secrets-sprawl-report-2024

===== PAGE 19 =====
What If It Is Already Committed?
20
•
Assume it's public forever
•
Step 1: rotate the key now — revoke at the provider, regenerate
•
Step 2: audit downstream — CI, Vercel env, teammates' .env
“Walk me through rotating my OpenAI key at platform.openai.com, then audit usage and clean history.”

===== PAGE 28 =====
Aim for SLC, not MVP
29
•
Minimum Viable Product = "ugly but functional"
•
Simple, Lovable, Complete = "small but delightful"
•
++ With AI, finishing is cheap now — MVP's excuse is outdated
•
One polished feature > five half-done ones
•
Coined by Jason Cohen (2017) — fits the vibe-coding era [1]
[1] https://longform.asmartbear.com/slc/

===== PAGE 29 =====
Mine Your Own Patterns — `/insights`
30
•
Built-in Claude Code command — analyzes your last 30 days of sessions [1]
•
Produces an HTML report: usage stats, friction points, behavioral patterns
•
++ The goldmine: instructions you repeat often → paste into CLAUDE.md
•
Runs locally — no data leaves your machine
•
Run it after each milestone; monthly at minimum
•
Your CLAUDE.md gets smarter without you guessing what's missing
[1] https://code.claude.com/docs/en/commands.md
